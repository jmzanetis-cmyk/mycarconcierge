Every service provider on My Car Concierge goes through a comprehensive verification process before they can bid on your service requests. We take provider quality seriously.

  • Identity Verification: Government ID verification ensures providers are who they claim to be.
  • Background Checks (Optional): Providers can voluntarily complete background screenings. Look for the "Background Verified" badge on provider profiles.
  • License Verification: We verify professional licenses and certifications where applicable.
  • Insurance Confirmation: Providers must maintain appropriate liability insurance coverage.
  • Business Verification: For business accounts, we verify legitimate business registration.
  • Ongoing Monitoring: Provider status is continuously monitored, not just at sign-up.

üí∞ Secure Escrow System

Our escrow payment system protects both vehicle owners and service providers by ensuring funds are handled fairly throughout every transaction.

  • Payment Protection: Your payment is held securely until you confirm the work is complete.
  • Funds Release: Money is only released to providers after you verify satisfaction.
  • Secure Processing: All payments are processed through industry-standard encryption.
  • Clear Timeline: Both parties know exactly when and how funds will be released.
  • No Advance Payments: Providers don't receive payment until the job is done right.

In the rare event that something goes wrong, our fair and structured dispute resolution process ensures both parties are heard and treated fairly.

  • Direct Resolution: We encourage and facilitate direct communication between parties first.
  • Mediation Support: Our team can step in to help mediate if direct resolution isn't working.
  • Evidence Review: We review photos, messages, and documentation from both sides.
  • Fair Decisions: Impartial decisions based on our terms of service and the facts presented.
  • Refund Protection: If warranted, we can process refunds from the escrow funds.
  • Provider Accountability: Providers with unresolved issues face account restrictions.
