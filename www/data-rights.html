Your Privacy Choices

Exercise your data rights and manage how My Car Concierge uses your personal information.

Your Data Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal information:

📄

Right to Know

Request details about what personal data we collect, use, and share.

🗑

Right to Delete

Ask us to delete personal information we have collected about you.

✏

Right to Correct

Request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold.

🗎

Right to Opt Out

Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising.

⚖

Right to Portability

Receive a copy of your personal data in a portable, usable format.

🚫

Right to Limit

Limit how we use sensitive personal information beyond core service needs.

🚫 Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

My Car Concierge does not sell your personal information for money. We may share certain data with advertising or analytics partners in ways that may constitute "sharing" under California law (CCPA/CPRA).

Use the form below to submit a request to opt out of any such sharing. Once processed, we will update our records within 15 business days.

Submit a Privacy Request

Complete this form to exercise any of your privacy rights. We will respond within 45 days as required by law. You may be contacted to verify your identity.

Response Timeline

  • We will acknowledge your request within 10 business days.
  • We will complete your request within 45 calendar days. We may extend this by an additional 45 days for complex requests, with notice.
  • We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Requests may be denied if we cannot verify your identity or if an exception applies.
