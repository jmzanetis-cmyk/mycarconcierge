How Bidding Works

  • Each bid submission costs one credit from your purchased pack
  • Credits never expire - use them at your own pace
  • Win rate varies by service category and your reviews
  • Unused credits are refundable if your account is suspended
  • Founding Providers receive unlimited bidding during pilot period
  • Higher tier packs include priority support
