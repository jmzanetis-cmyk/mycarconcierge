To become a verified provider, create an account and complete our verification process. You'll need to provide:

• Valid government-issued ID for identity verification
• Proof of relevant licenses and certifications
• Proof of liability insurance coverage
• Business registration documents (if applicable)

Our team reviews applications within 2-3 business days. Once approved, you'll be able to start bidding on service requests in your area.

My Car Concierge charges a small service fee on completed jobs. There are no upfront costs, monthly subscriptions, or fees to submit bids. You only pay when you successfully complete a job and receive payment.

The exact fee structure is outlined in our provider agreement during registration. This model ensures we're aligned with your success—we only earn when you earn.

When a vehicle owner accepts your bid, their payment is held in our secure escrow system. Once you complete the work and the customer confirms satisfaction, funds are released to your connected payout account.

We support direct bank transfers and other payment methods. Payouts are typically processed within 1-3 business days after the customer approves the completed work.
