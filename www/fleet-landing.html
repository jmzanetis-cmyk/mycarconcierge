Platform Features

Everything your fleet needs

Purpose-built tools that work together so nothing falls through the cracks.

🚗
Vehicle Management
Add, track, and organize all company vehicles with fleet numbers, departments, and assignment history. VIN tracking and maintenance scheduling built in.
👤
Driver Management
Invite drivers by email, assign roles and vehicles, set spending limits, and control which requests need approval before they go to a provider.
✅
Approval Workflows
Set auto-approve thresholds for small repairs. Anything above the limit lands in your queue for review before any work begins.
📊
Spending Analytics
See per-vehicle spend, category breakdowns, and month-over-month comparisons. Export reports in one click for accounting.
🔧
Bulk Service Scheduling
Schedule the same service for your entire fleet or a subset of vehicles at once — oil changes, tire rotations, inspections, and more.

Driver Mobile Portal
Drivers get their own mobile-first view of their assigned vehicle, service history, and a simple way to report issues to the fleet manager.
