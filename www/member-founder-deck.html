What Is My Car Concierge?
A Three-Sided Marketplace: Members · Providers · Drivers
The Concept
My Car Concierge is a three-sided automotive marketplace connecting car owners, service providers, and professional drivers. Members get competing bids from verified automotive professionals. Providers grow their customer base with built-in vehicle pickup & delivery — a differentiator that wins more bids at a fraction of Google Ads cost. Drivers earn $35–50/hr keeping 75% of every trip.
