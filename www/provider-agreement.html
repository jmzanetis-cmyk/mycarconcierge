1Introduction

This Provider Agreement ("Agreement") is a legally binding contract between you ("Provider", "you", or "your") and My Car Concierge, LLC ("MCC", "we", "us", or "our"). By registering as a Provider on the MCC platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Agreement.

MCC operates a marketplace platform that connects vehicle owners ("Members") with independent automotive service providers. As a Provider, you will have the opportunity to bid on service requests posted by Members and, if selected, perform automotive services directly for those Members.

This Agreement governs your relationship with MCC as an independent contractor using our platform. Your relationship with Members is separate and governed by the terms you agree upon with each Member for specific services.

2Provider Eligibility

To register and maintain a Provider account on MCC, you must meet and continuously maintain the following requirements:

  • Age Requirement: You must be at least 18 years of age
  • Business Authorization: You must be legally authorized to operate a business in your jurisdiction
  • Professional Licensing: You must hold all licenses, certifications, and permits required by applicable federal, state, and local laws for the services you offer (e.g., automotive repair license, emissions testing certification, etc.)
  • Insurance Coverage: You must maintain general liability insurance with minimum coverage of $1,000,000 per occurrence and provide proof of coverage upon request
  • Vehicle Insurance: If providing mobile services, you must maintain commercial auto insurance
  • Background Check: You may be required to pass a background check at MCC's discretion
  • Accurate Information: All information provided in your application must be accurate, current, and complete

Failure to maintain these eligibility requirements at any time may result in immediate suspension or termination of your Provider account.

3Independent Contractor Status

YOU ARE AN INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR, NOT AN EMPLOYEE. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, agency, partnership, or joint venture relationship between you and MCC.

As an independent contractor:

  • You have complete control over when, where, and how you perform services
  • You are free to accept or reject any bid opportunity on the platform
  • You may work for competing platforms and maintain other business relationships
  • You are solely responsible for paying all applicable taxes, including self-employment tax, income tax, and any other taxes required by law
  • You are responsible for providing your own tools, equipment, and supplies
  • You are not entitled to any employee benefits from MCC, including health insurance, retirement plans, workers' compensation, or paid time off
  • You must comply with all applicable laws regarding independent contractor status

MCC does not control the manner or method by which you perform your services. MCC's role is limited to providing the technology platform that connects you with Members.

4Services and Standards

You are solely responsible for the quality, safety, and legality of all services you provide through the MCC platform. You agree to:

  • Perform all services in a professional, workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards
  • Complete all accepted jobs as described in your bid and agreed upon with the Member
  • Use only quality parts and materials appropriate for the service being performed
  • Accurately represent your qualifications, experience, and capabilities in your profile
  • Only bid on services you are qualified and legally permitted to perform
  • Communicate promptly and professionally with Members regarding scheduling, progress, and any issues
  • Arrive on time for scheduled appointments or provide advance notice if delays occur
  • Leave the work area clean and dispose of all waste materials properly
  • Provide warranties on your work as required by law or as agreed with the Member

MCC does NOT inspect, verify, or guarantee the quality of your work. Members rely on your professional reputation and the ratings/reviews system.

5Bid Credits and Bidding

The MCC platform uses a Bid Credit system for Providers to submit bids on Member service requests.

  • Purchasing Bid Credits: Bid Credits are purchased in packages through the platform. Pricing is displayed at the time of purchase.
  • Using Bid Credits: Each bid you submit on a service request consumes one or more Bid Credits, depending on the service category.
  • Bid Submission: When you submit a bid, you are making a binding offer to perform the described services at the quoted price.
  • Member Selection: Members review bids and select a Provider at their discretion. MCC does not influence or guarantee Member selection.

BID CREDITS ARE NON-REFUNDABLE. Once purchased, Bid Credits cannot be refunded, exchanged for cash, or transferred to another account, regardless of whether your bids are accepted by Members.

  • Bid Credits do not expire as long as your account remains active and in good standing
  • If your account is terminated for any reason, all unused Bid Credits are forfeited
  • MCC reserves the right to modify Bid Credit pricing with 30 days notice to existing Providers

6Payment Terms

All payments on the MCC platform are processed through Stripe, our third-party payment processor. By using the platform, you agree to Stripe's terms of service.

  • Job Pricing: You set your own prices when submitting bids. MCC does not set or control your pricing.
  • Platform Fee: MCC charges a platform fee on completed jobs, which is deducted from your payment. The current platform fee is disclosed in your Provider dashboard and during the bidding process.
  • Payment Hold: When a Member accepts your bid, the payment is held in escrow until the job is completed.
  • Payment Release: Funds are released to your connected Stripe account after the Member confirms job completion or after the applicable review period (typically 48 hours after marking the job complete).
  • Payout Timing: Once released, funds are transferred to your bank account according to Stripe's standard payout schedule (typically 2-3 business days).
  • Stripe Account: You must connect a valid Stripe account to receive payments. You are responsible for keeping your Stripe account in good standing.

You are solely responsible for all taxes on income earned through the platform. MCC will provide applicable tax forms (e.g., 1099) as required by law.

7Ratings and Reviews

The MCC platform includes a ratings and reviews system that allows Members to rate Providers after completed services. This system is essential to maintaining platform quality and Member trust.

  • Rating Scale: Members rate Providers on a 1-5 star scale after each completed job
  • Public Display: Your average rating and reviews are displayed publicly on your Provider profile
  • Response Option: You may respond professionally to reviews through the platform

SUSPENSION POLICY: Providers who maintain an average rating below 4.0 stars after receiving 3 or more reviews may have their account suspended. Repeated low ratings may result in permanent termination.

Suspension due to low ratings may be appealed by contacting MCC support. Reinstatement is at MCC's sole discretion and may require completion of additional training or verification.

8Provider Conduct

As a Provider on the MCC platform, you agree to conduct yourself professionally at all times. You must:

  • Treat all Members, MCC staff, and other Providers with respect and courtesy
  • Communicate professionally through the platform and in person
  • Never use threatening, harassing, discriminatory, or abusive language or behavior
  • Not solicit Members to conduct transactions outside of the MCC platform
  • Not offer or accept cash payments or other off-platform payments for jobs initiated on MCC
  • Maintain appropriate professional appearance when meeting with Members
  • Never misrepresent your identity, qualifications, or the nature of services provided
  • Protect Member privacy and not share their personal information with third parties
  • Report any safety concerns or suspicious activity to MCC immediately

Violations of this conduct policy may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent termination of your account at MCC's sole discretion.

You understand and agree that MCC does not provide any insurance coverage for your services. Any claims arising from your services are your sole responsibility.

11Termination

This Agreement may be terminated as follows:

  • By You: You may terminate this Agreement at any time by deactivating your Provider account through the platform
  • By MCC Without Cause: MCC may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, with or without notice

  • MCC may withhold payment pending resolution of disputes at its sole discretion

MCC encourages professional resolution of disputes. Providers who frequently have unresolved disputes may be subject to account review and potential suspension.

For disputes between you and MCC regarding this Agreement or the platform, you agree to first attempt resolution through MCC's support channels. If unresolved, disputes will be settled by binding arbitration in accordance with the American Arbitration Association rules.
