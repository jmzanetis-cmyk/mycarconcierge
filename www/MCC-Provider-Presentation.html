What Is My Car Concierge?
A New Way to Connect with Customers
The Concept
My Car Concierge is a marketplace that connects vehicle owners (members) with professional automotive service providers. We're not a replacement for your business‚Äîwe're a tool to help you grow it.
How It Works
  1. Members post service requests describing what they need
  2. Providers receive notifications and review the job details
  3. Providers submit competitive bids on jobs they want
  4. Members choose their preferred provider based on price, reviews, and proximity
  5. Work is completed and payment is released from escrow
Your Advantage
Think of it this way: Every customer you bring onto MCC becomes part of a shared pool. When they need a service you don't offer, another provider wins. When other providers' customers need YOUR specialty, you win.

Pay Only for Jobs You Choose

How Ratings Work
After every completed job, members rate their experience on a 5-star scale and can leave written feedback. Your rating directly impacts your visibility and success on the platform.
Quality Standards
  • All reviews are from verified, completed transactions
  • Providers can respond publicly to reviews
  • High-rated providers get priority visibility in search results

Team Management
Add Your Staff with Role-Based Access
Multi-User Access
Your MCC provider account isn't limited to one person. Add team members so multiple people can manage jobs, respond to customers, and update vehicle status.
Team Roles
Owner
Full access including billing, team management, and account settings. Can add/remove team members and purchase bid credits.
Admin
Manage jobs, respond to messages, update vehicle status, view analytics. Cannot modify billing or team.
Staff
Update job status, communicate with members on assigned jobs. Limited dashboard access.
How to Add Team Members
  1. Go to Team Management in your provider dashboard
  2. Enter the team member's email address
  3. Select their role (Admin or Staff)
  4. They receive an invitation email to join
  5. Once accepted, they can log in with their own credentials
Audit Trail
All actions are logged with the team member who performed them. Know who updated a job status or sent a message at any time.

Platform Protections
Fair Practices for Everyone
A Level Playing Field
MCC enforces rules that protect both members and honest providers. Bad actors are removed quickly, and fair practices are rewarded.
Member Protections (That Help You)
  • Anti-Bait Protection: No "free diagnostic" upselling tactics. Honest pricing from the start builds trust in the platform.
  • Upsell Approval: Any additional work beyond the original bid must be approved by the member in-app. No surprise charges.
  • 24-Hour Window: Members have time to review and approve additional work. No high-pressure decisions.
  • Verified Reviews: Only completed transactions can leave reviews. No fake reviews from competitors.
Provider Protections
  • Escrow Payments: Funds secured before work begins. No non-payment risk.
  • Dispute Resolution: MCC mediates disputes fairly with evidence from both sides.
  • Identity Verification: Members verify their identity, reducing fraud risk.
  • No-Show Protection: Members who repeatedly no-show are flagged.

How to Get Started
Simple Onboarding Process
Application Steps
  1. Submit Application: Fill out the online form with your business information, services offered, and service area
  2. Verification: MCC reviews your application and verifies your business credentials
  3. Account Setup: Once approved, set up your provider profile with photos, description, and service details
  4. Add Team: Invite any team members who will help manage jobs
  5. Start Bidding: Begin receiving job notifications and submitting bids
What You'll Need
  • Business name and contact information
  • Service categories you offer
  • Service area (zip codes or radius)
  • Business license or proof of operation (if applicable)
  • Bank account for receiving payments
Typical Timeline
Most applications are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Once approved, you can be bidding on jobs the same day.
