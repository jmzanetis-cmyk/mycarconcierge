How My Car Concierge Works

Post what you need. Compare bids from vetted providers. Choose with confidence. Here's the complete walkthrough.

1

Create Your Account

Getting started is free and straightforward. Set up your member account to access the platform's full capabilities. Add your vehicles with their specific details so providers understand exactly what they're servicing.

  • Sign up for free with email or phone
  • Add your vehicles (make, model, year, photos)
  • Verify ownership with registration document (optional but builds trust)
  • Complete your profile with location and preferences
2

Post a Service Request

Describe exactly what your car needs and let qualified providers bid on your job. The more detail you provide, the more accurate the bids you'll receive. Specify your requirements to get competitive pricing and reliable timelines.

  • Describe what your car needs (maintenance, repairs, detailing, body work)
  • Add photos to help providers understand the job scope
  • Set your location and preferred availability
3

Receive Competitive Bids

Vetted providers in your area review your request and submit bids within hours. Each bid includes transparent pricing, realistic timelines, and information about the provider's qualifications. You control the conversation every step of the way.

  • Vetted providers in your area see your request
  • They submit competitive bids with pricing and timeline
  • Review provider profiles, ratings, and specialties
  • Ask questions directly through the platform messaging
4

Choose Your Provider

Compare all bids side-by-side to make an informed decision. Your funds are held securely in escrow, protecting both you and the service provider. Payment only releases when you confirm the job is complete to your satisfaction.

  • Compare bids side-by-side with all details
  • Select the provider that best fits your needs and budget
  • Funds held securely in escrow during the service
  • If additional work is discovered, you get 4 hours to accept or decline the update
5

Get Service & Pay Securely

Your provider delivers the service while you stay informed throughout. Review the completed work, confirm everything meets your expectations, and release payment with confidence. Leave a review to help the community find trusted providers.

  • Schedule your appointment at a convenient time
  • Track vehicle transfers if using pickup/delivery service
  • Confirm job completion to release payment
  • Leave a review to help other members make informed choices

Beyond Service: Additional Platform Features

My Car Concierge offers more than just service requests. Manage your automotive needs comprehensively with these additional tools:

  • Vehicle Management – Track maintenance reminders, recalls, service history, and important documents all in one place
  • Vehicle Maintenance – Learn about your vehicle with educational resources to understand maintenance needs and care best practices
  • Dream Car Finder – Shop for your next vehicle with tools to compare options and connect with sellers
