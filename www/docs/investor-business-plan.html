A $443B Industry Running on Phone Calls

The automotive aftermarket is one of the largest consumer markets in America—yet it operates like it's 1995.

Call Shop B "Bring it in, we'll take a look"
Call Shop C Voicemail...

Give up Overpay at the first shop that answers

1. Two-Sided Network Effects

More providers means better pricing, which attracts more members, which attracts more providers. Winner-take-most dynamics.

Unlike Yelp/Thumbtack, we hold funds until completion. Creates trust layer that enables transactions.

3. Complete Vehicle Ecosystem

Competitors focus on ONE thing. We own the entire vehicle ownership relationship.

4. Viral Referral Economics

50% lifetime commission creates army of recruiters. Drastically reduces CAC.
