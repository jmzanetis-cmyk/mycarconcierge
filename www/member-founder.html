How Commissions Work

Simple, Transparent Payouts

You earn 50% of every bid-credit purchase made by providers you refer — for 12 months from your enrollment date. Commissions are held for a 7-day review window, then released for the next monthly payout on the 1st.

Earnings disclosure: Commission income depends entirely on how many providers you refer and how many bid credits they purchase. Individual results will vary. MCC makes no guarantee of any specific income level. This is not an employment relationship — you participate as an independent referral partner.

Commissions go through two stacked steps before you receive payment. First, each commission is held for 7 days after it is earned — this holding period exists so that any provider refund or payment dispute can be processed before the commission is released. Once a commission clears the 7-day window it moves to payable status. Second, payable commissions are disbursed in a single monthly payout run on the 1st of each month. This means a commission earned just after the 1st could be up to ~38 days from earned to paid (7-day hold + up to ~31 days to the next payout run). A commission earned shortly before the 1st will arrive much sooner. Balances below the $25 minimum roll over to the next month.
