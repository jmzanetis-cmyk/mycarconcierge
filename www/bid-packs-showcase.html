How Bidding Works

You Choose Your Jobs

Browse available service requests and only bid on jobs that fit your shop's capabilities and schedule. You're never obligated to bid.

One Credit Per Bid

Each bid you submit uses one credit from your pack. If you win the job, you get paid. If not, you've invested in visibility.

Escrow Protection

When a member accepts your bid, funds are held in escrow. Complete the work, get confirmation, and receive guaranteed payment.

Build Your Reputation

Every completed job earns you a rating. High ratings mean better visibility and more wins on future bids.
