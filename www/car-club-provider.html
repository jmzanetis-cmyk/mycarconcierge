Car Club Management

Build loyalty and reward your best customers

🏁 How Your Club Works

Members auto-join your club after their first completed job with you. They earn punches with each visit and unlock rewards once they hit your set threshold.

⭐ Setting Up Rewards

Create punch cards (up to 3 active at a time), set the number of punches required, and choose what members earn when they complete a card.

👥 What Members See

Members can see their punch progress, view available rewards, and browse or join your club directly from their dashboard.

💡 Tips for Success

Set achievable punch counts (5–10 is ideal), offer meaningful rewards that keep customers coming back, and engage with your club members regularly.
