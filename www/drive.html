How it works

Three steps to lifetime income.

You already talk to shops, dealerships, and independent mechanics every day. Now every referral pays you — forever.

1

Apply in the MCC Driver app

Sign up as a driver, then open the Founder tab and submit your one-time application. Takes under two minutes — just your name, location, and a short note about how you plan to refer providers.

2

Get your referral code & QR

Once approved you'll receive a unique referral code and a downloadable QR code. Share it however you work best — text, DM, printed on a card, a sticker on your dash.

3

Earn 50% every time they buy bid credits

When a provider you referred buys bid credits to bid on member jobs, you earn 50% of their purchase — automatically, every time, for as long as they're on the platform. No cap. No expiration.

What does "50% for life" actually look like?

Here's a conservative scenario with three active provider referrals.

Commissions are held for 7 days, then paid on the 1st of each month via Stripe Connect or PayPal. Balances below $25 roll to the next cycle. These numbers are illustrative — actual provider spend varies.

Disclosure: My Car Concierge Driver Founders earn a commission when a provider they referred purchases bid credits on the MCC platform. This is a material financial relationship. When sharing your referral code, you must disclose this relationship clearly — for example: "I earn a commission if you purchase bid credits using my referral code."
