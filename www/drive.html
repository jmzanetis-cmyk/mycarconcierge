How it works

Three steps to lifetime income.

You already know providers — dealerships, independent mechanics, mobile techs, detailers. Now every referral pays you — forever.

1

Apply in the MCC Driver app

Sign up as a driver, then open the Founder tab and submit your one-time application. Takes under two minutes — just your name, location, and a short note about how you plan to refer providers.

2

Get your referral code & QR

Once approved you'll receive a unique referral code and a downloadable QR code. Share it however you work best — text, DM, printed on a card, a sticker on your dash.

3

Earn 50% every time they buy bid credits

When a provider you referred buys bid credits to bid on member jobs, you earn 50% of their purchase — every time, for as long as they're on the platform. No cap. No expiration. Once you've made the referral, their ongoing activity generates recurring commissions for you — income that continues without repeat referral work, provided they keep buying credits.

Commission mechanics

What "50% for life" actually means.

Your commission rate 50%
Earned when A referred provider buys bid credits
Payout schedule Monthly (1st of month)
Hold period 7 days per transaction
Minimum payout $25 (rolls to next cycle)

Commissions are paid via Stripe Connect or PayPal.

What you can realistically expect: Your earnings depend entirely on how many providers you refer and whether they buy bid credits. Individual results vary — there is no guaranteed income. Once a provider you referred is active, their ongoing bid-credit purchases generate recurring commissions for you without additional referral work on your part — a form of passive income contingent on their continued platform activity.
Disclosure: My Car Concierge Driver Founders earn a commission when a provider they referred purchases bid credits on the MCC platform. This is a material financial relationship. When sharing your referral code, you must disclose this relationship clearly — for example: "I earn a commission if you purchase bid credits using my referral code."
