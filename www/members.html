When your check engine light comes on, your car stores diagnostic codes like P0420 or P0171. You can get these read for free at AutoZone, O'Reilly, or use a scanner like FIXD.

Separate multiple codes with commas. Codes start with P, C, B, or U followed by 4 digits.
