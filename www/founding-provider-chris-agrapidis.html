FOUNDING PROVIDER PARTNER

Founding Provider Partner Agreement

Effective Date: January 23, 2026

BETWEEN: Zanetis Holdings LLC d/b/a My Car Concierge, 107 Almond Drive, Somerset, NJ 08873 ("MCC")

AND: Chris Agrapidis ("Founding Provider")

1. Founding Provider Benefits

1.1 Zero Fees. Founding Provider receives unlimited bid credits at no cost and pays zero transaction fees. Founding Provider keeps 100% of customer payments minus only payment processing fees.

1.2 Referral Commissions. Founding Provider receives 90% of all bid pack purchases made by any provider they refer to MCC, for the lifetime of that provider's account. Commissions paid monthly within 15 business days.

1.3 Milestone Bonuses. Founding Provider receives one-time bonuses when MCC achieves platform-wide bid pack sales milestones:

Milestone Sales Bonus
$25,000 $2,500
$50,000 $5,000
$100,000 $12,500
$250,000 $30,000
$500,000 $60,000
$1,000,000 $125,000

Bonuses continue at 12.5% of each milestone achieved beyond $1,000,000. Additional milestone amounts and bonus structures may be established by mutual written agreement as the platform grows.

2. Founding Provider Obligations

2.1 Service Standards. Founding Provider shall maintain required licenses, insurance, and certifications, and comply with all applicable laws.

2.2 Platform Compliance. Founding Provider shall not conduct off-platform transactions with MCC customers, shall maintain accurate profile information, and provide timely job status updates.

2.3 Provider Recruitment. Founding Provider shall actively recruit qualified service providers to the MCC platform.

3. Terms & Conditions

3.1 Independent Contractor. Founding Provider is an independent contractor, not an employee. MCC will issue Form 1099-NEC for payments exceeding $600/year. Founding Provider provides Form W-9 before first payment and is responsible for all taxes, insurance, and business expenses.

3.2 Duration. This Agreement continues indefinitely and may only be terminated by mutual written agreement of both parties.

3.3 Commission Protection. The 90% commission rate on already-referred providers continues for life, even if this Agreement terminates. All other benefits (zero fees, milestone bonuses) end upon termination.

3.4 Confidentiality. Both parties maintain confidentiality of business information, customer data, and proprietary processes.

3.5 Intellectual Property. All MCC trademarks, platform technology, and IP remain MCC's exclusive property. Founding Provider may use MCC branding only for recruiting providers.

3.6 Insurance. Founding Provider maintains commercial general liability ($1M), automotive liability (state minimums), and professional liability ($500K) insurance.

3.7 Modification. This Agreement may only be modified by written agreement signed by both parties.

3.8 Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by New Jersey law. Disputes resolved first through negotiation, then mediation, then New Jersey courts.
