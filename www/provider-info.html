How It Works

Apply & Get Verified

Complete our application with your business credentials, licenses, and insurance. Our team reviews every application to ensure quality providers.

Receive Service Requests

Once approved, you'll see service requests from members in your area. Browse opportunities that match your specialties and availability.

Submit Competitive Bids

Review the job details and submit your bid. Include your timeline and any relevant notes. Members choose based on price, ratings, and your profile.

Complete the Work

Once selected, coordinate with the member, complete the service to your high standards, and mark the job complete in your dashboard.

Get Paid

Payment is released from escrow within 3-5 business days of job completion. Simple, secure, and reliable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the fees?

There is no platform fee on completed jobs — you keep 100% of what members pay you, minus standard Stripe payment processing (2.9% + 30¢ per transaction). There are no signup fees and no monthly subscription. The only thing you pay us for is bid-credit packs, which you use when you want to compete for a job — that's how the platform makes its money.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed via direct deposit within 3-5 business days of job completion. Funds are held in escrow from the moment a member accepts your bid.

What if a customer disputes the work?

Our support team mediates any disputes fairly. We review all communication and evidence to reach a resolution. Quality providers with good documentation rarely face issues.

Can I choose which jobs to bid on?

Absolutely. You have full control over which service requests you pursue. Bid on jobs that match your expertise, availability, and pricing preferences.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are reviewed within 2-3 business days. We verify your credentials, licenses, and insurance to maintain our quality standards.
