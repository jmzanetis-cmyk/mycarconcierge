Frequently Asked Questions

What are the fees?

There is no platform fee on completed jobs — you keep 100% of what members pay you, minus standard Stripe payment processing (2.9% + 30¢ per transaction). There are no signup fees and no monthly subscription. The only thing you pay us for is bid-credit packs, which you use when you want to compete for a job — that's how the platform makes its money.

How do I get paid?

When a member accepts your bid, their payment is authorized and held in escrow — no money leaves their account upfront. Once you complete the job and the member confirms, the funds are captured and deposited to your bank account within 3-5 business days via Stripe Connect.

What are crowd-funded jobs?

After a member selects your winning bid, they have the option to crowd-fund the job — meaning they can split the cost among family, friends, or others. You'll see a "Crowd Funded" badge on these jobs. Don't schedule the work until the job is fully funded — you'll receive a notification when all payments are collected and it's safe to begin. The escrow guarantee still applies: funds are held securely until the job is complete.

What if a customer disputes the work?

Our support team mediates any disputes fairly. We review all communication and evidence to reach a resolution. Quality providers with good documentation rarely face issues.

Can I choose which jobs to bid on?

Absolutely. You have full control over which service requests you pursue. Bid on jobs that match your expertise, availability, and pricing preferences.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are reviewed within 2-3 business days. We verify your credentials, licenses, and insurance to maintain our quality standards.
