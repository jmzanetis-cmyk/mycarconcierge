My Car Concierge
iOS App Store Submission Guide

App Name: My Car Concierge
Bundle ID: co.mycarconcierge.app
Website: https://mycarconcierge.com

Requirements: You need a Mac computer with Xcode installed. Apple does not allow iOS apps to be built on Windows or Linux.

1 Set Up Your Mac

Download and install the following on your Mac:

  • Xcode - Free from the Mac App Store (search for "Xcode")
  • Node.js - Download from nodejs.org (LTS version)
  • CocoaPods - Open Terminal and run: sudo gem install cocoapods

2 Download Your Project

Copy your entire My Car Concierge project folder from Replit to your Mac. You can:

  • Use the "Download as ZIP" option in Replit
  • Or clone from your Git repository if you have one set up

Extract the files to a folder on your Mac (e.g., ~/Projects/mycarconcierge)

3 Install Dependencies

Open Terminal on your Mac and navigate to your project folder:

cd ~/Projects/mycarconcierge
npm install
cd ios/App
pod install
cd ../..
Note: If you get permission errors with CocoaPods, try: sudo pod install

4 Open in Xcode

Run this command to open your project in Xcode:

npx cap open ios

This will launch Xcode with your iOS project loaded.

5 Configure Code Signing

In Xcode:

  1. Click on "App" in the left sidebar (the project name)
  2. Select the "App" target
  3. Click the "Signing & Capabilities" tab
  4. Check the box for "Automatically manage signing"
  5. In the "Team" dropdown, select your Apple Developer account
  6. Xcode will automatically create the necessary certificates
Success: If you see a green checkmark next to "Signing Certificate," you're ready to build!

6 Build (Archive) Your App

  1. At the top of Xcode, click on the device selector (where it might say "iPhone 15 Pro")
  2. Select "Any iOS Device (arm64)" or "Generic iOS Device"
  3. From the menu bar, go to Product â†’ Archive
  4. Wait for the build to complete (this may take several minutes)
  5. When done, the Organizer window will open automatically

7 Upload to App Store Connect

In the Organizer window:

  1. Select your archive from the list
  2. Click "Distribute App"
  3. Select "App Store Connect" and click Next
  4. Select "Upload" and click Next
  5. Keep the default options checked and click Next
  6. Review the info and click "Upload"
  7. Wait for the upload to complete
Note: The upload may take 10-30 minutes depending on your internet connection.

8 Create Your App in App Store Connect

Go to appstoreconnect.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

  1. Click "My Apps"
  2. Click the "+" button and select "New App"

You'll need to provide the following information:

Pricing and Availability

10 Select Your Build and Submit

  1. Go to the "App Store" tab in App Store Connect
  2. Scroll down to the "Build" section
  3. Click the "+" to select your uploaded build
  4. Your build should appear after processing (30-60 minutes after upload)
  5. Select your build
  6. Complete the Export Compliance questions
  7. Choose your release option:
    • Automatically release - App goes live when approved
    • Manually release - You control when it goes live
  8. Click "Add for Review"
  9. Review the submission checklist
  10. Click "Submit for Review"

What Happens Next?

Review Time: Apple typically reviews apps within 24-48 hours, though it can take up to 7 days.

You'll receive email notifications about:

  • When your app is in review
  • When your app is approved (or if changes are needed)
  • When your app is live on the App Store

Common Rejection Reasons to Avoid

  • Crashes or bugs - Test thoroughly before submitting
  • Placeholder content - Remove any "Lorem ipsum" or test data
  • Missing privacy policy - Make sure your privacy policy URL works
  • Poor screenshots - Use high-quality, accurate screenshots
  • Incomplete features - All features should work properly
