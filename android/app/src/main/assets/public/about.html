Our Story

The Problem We Saw

Vehicle owners face a common frustration: finding reliable automotive service at a fair price. Too often, they're left guessing whether they're getting a good deal, wondering if the mechanic is trustworthy, or struggling to find available appointments that fit their schedule.

Meanwhile, skilled service providers‚Äîfrom independent mechanics to mobile detailers‚Äîstruggle to find new customers and compete with large chains that dominate advertising.

The Solution We Built

My Car Concierge was founded with a simple idea: what if we could create a marketplace where vehicle owners post what they need, and qualified providers compete for their business?

This model puts the power back in the hands of consumers. Instead of calling around for quotes or hoping for the best, you can see multiple bids side-by-side, compare provider ratings and qualifications, and choose with confidence.

For providers, it means access to a steady stream of customers actively seeking services, without the massive advertising budgets required to compete traditionally.

Our Vision

A world where finding automotive service is as easy as ordering a rideshare: transparent pricing, verified providers, and protection for everyone involved. We're building the future of how people care for their vehicles.
