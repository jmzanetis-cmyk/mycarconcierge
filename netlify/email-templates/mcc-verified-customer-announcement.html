We're excited to introduce MCC Verified — a new way to find providers you can trust on My Car Concierge.

Look for the ✓ Background Verified badge when browsing providers. It means at least 90% of that provider's customer-facing employees have passed a comprehensive background check through our nationally accredited screening partner — and those checks are renewed every year.

What's screened:
• National criminal history search
• County-level criminal records
• National sex offender registry
• Identity verification

You can now filter for Verified Providers in the directory using the new "Show only Verified Providers" toggle.
